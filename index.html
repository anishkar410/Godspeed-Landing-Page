Why Choose Godspeed?

Godspeed provides everything you need to build modern microservices with ease and efficiency.

Lightning Fast Development

Generate APIs in minutes with our powerful CLI and scaffolding tools. Focus on business logic while we handle the boilerplate.

Built-in Security

Enterprise-grade security with JWT, OAuth2, and role-based access control out of the box. Sleep easy knowing your APIs are protected.

Extensible Architecture

Connect to any datasource with our plugin system. Already support for SQL, NoSQL, REST, GraphQL, and more.

Event-Driven

About Godspeed

Godspeed was born out of frustration with the complexity of building and maintaining microservices architectures. As developers ourselves, we wanted to create a framework that would make it easy to build scalable, secure, and maintainable microservices without the usual headaches.

Our mission is to empower developers to focus on what matters most - building great products. By abstracting away the complexity of infrastructure, security, and scalability, we enable teams to move faster and deliver more value to their users.

Today, Godspeed is trusted by startups and enterprises alike to power their mission-critical applications. Our vibrant community of contributors continues to grow, making Godspeed better every day.
